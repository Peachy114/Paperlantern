Later N Comix Storyteller Agreement
By becoming a Storyteller on Later N Comix, you agree to the following:
1. Content Standards

You must not upload sexually explicit or suggestive content, especially involving minors
You must not upload content that promotes hate speech, discrimination, or harassment
You must not plagiarize or upload content you do not own
Comic-style drawn violence is permitted within reason, but real photographs or realistic depictions of gore, murder, or graphic injury are strictly prohibited

2. Earnings

You earn credits when readers purchase your locked chapters
Later N Comix takes a platform fee from each transaction
Earnings are withdrawable once you reach the minimum threshold

3. Merchandise Advertising

You may advertise your own merchandise below chapter images
Ads must be relevant to your work and not misleading
Later N Comix reserves the right to remove ads that violate guidelines

4. Violations & Consequences

Minor violations may result in content removal or temporary suspension
Severe violations — especially uploading real photographs of gore, murder, or graphic violence — will result in permanent account removal with no appeal
Later N Comix reserves the right to update these terms at any time
